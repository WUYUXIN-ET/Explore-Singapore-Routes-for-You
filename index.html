Singapore -
City Flavors and Cultural Roaming Guide

Singapore - City Flavors and Cultural Roaming Guide is a data display showcasing the distribution of cultural landmarks, dining establishments, and artistic experiences in Singapore, helping tourists and citizens explore the diversity and unique charm of the city more conveniently.

The storymap can promote the development of the tourism industry, provide users with a way to understand Singapore's modern food culture, and provide valuable data support for businesses to choose store locations. By analyzing the demand for catering and cultural hotspots, it helps to understand the characteristics of food culture in different regions.

The immersive experience mapcombining these three provides a comprehensive urban exploration route, encouraging users to deeply experience Singapore's culture and cuisine, promoting the integration of culture and consumption space, and also providing insights for urban planners to help optimize the distribution of cultural resources in the city.
